Zyft Health

AI health-tracking platform partnering with small and mid-sized gyms in Adelaide.

Visit Zyft Website -> Back to Work
[01] Context

Enterprise gym chains have access to analytics, member-retention tooling, and structured health tracking that smaller operators often cannot justify or maintain. Zyft Health is aimed at filling that gap for small and mid-sized gyms that want better member insight without enterprise overhead.

The core idea is to make health progress easier to log, review, and act on, so coaches and members can see what is changing before motivation disappears.

[02] Role & Scope

I am responsible for the product direction and execution shape: product strategy, UX, frontend development, partnerships, and project delivery.

  • Define the first MVP around the smallest useful coaching loops.
  • Translate gym-operator conversations into product requirements.
  • Coordinate frontend implementation and pilot-readiness milestones.
  • Prepare early Adelaide partnership conversations.
[03] Approach

Research

Early discovery is focused on conversations with gym operators, coaches, and likely users to understand what they already track, what they ignore, and where progress reviews become too manual.

Product

The MVP is structured around three loops: logging, weekly review, and coach feedback. Each loop has to earn its place by reducing friction rather than adding another dashboard to maintain.

Build

The active product stack is Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind, and Python, with the machine-learning layer still being scoped around what is genuinely useful in early pilots.

Distribution

The first rollout path is deliberately local: one to two Adelaide gym pilots before broadening the product or feature surface.
